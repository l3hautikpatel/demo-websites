How It Works

Four Steps to Deployment

Getting your workforce on-site is straightforward. We handle the complexity so you can focus on operations.

01

Request Service

Submit a workforce request online, by phone, or via email. Describe the scope, timeline, and skills required.

02

Requirement Assessment

Our project managers evaluate your needs, matching the right certifications, experience levels, and headcount to your job requirements.

03

Workforce Deployment

Pre-screened, certified technicians are dispatched to your site — fully equipped, briefed on your safety protocols, and ready to work.

04

Job Completion

Work completed to spec, with full documentation and sign-off. We follow up post-project to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Our Standards

Safety & Certification

Every IronForce technician is fully certified, safety-trained, and compliant with provincial and federal industrial regulations.

Certified Professionals

All technicians hold valid trade certifications, including Red Seal Millwrights, Industrial Mechanics, and specialized equipment tickets.

Safety-First Approach

We enforce rigorous LOTO, WHMIS, working-at-heights, and confined-space protocols on every job, every time. Zero compromise.
