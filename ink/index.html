About Us

A Trusted Partner in
Industrial Coding

Amphosis Ink Solutions was founded with a single mission: to provide manufacturing industries with the highest-quality CIJ inks, coding systems, and technical expertise they need to maintain compliant, efficient, and uninterrupted production lines.

With over 15 years of experience supplying inks and consumables to factories across North America, we understand the real demands of industrial production — tight deadlines, substrate diversity, regulatory pressure, and zero tolerance for downtime.

Our team of industry-trained engineers and formulation specialists work directly with each client to ensure every ink, every printer, and every service contract is perfectly matched to their operation.
